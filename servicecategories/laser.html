Alexandrite Laser

755nm Wavelength

The Alexandrite wavelength offers high melanin absorption, making it ideal for lighter skin tones and delivering exceptional results for finer hair removal.

  • Optimal for Fitzpatrick skin types I-III
  • Perfect for facial hair and delicate areas
  • Excellent for fine and light-colored hair
  • Fast treatment times with large spot sizes
  • Superior pigmented lesion treatment

Nd:YAG Laser

1064nm Wavelength

The longer Nd:YAG wavelength penetrates deeper into the skin, making it the gold standard for darker skin tones and treating coarser, deeper hair follicles.

  • Optimal for Fitzpatrick skin types IV-VI
  • Ideal for coarse, deep-rooted hair
  • Safe for tanned or darker skin tones
  • Treats vascular lesions effectively
  • Deeper penetration for stubborn areas
