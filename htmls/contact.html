FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit

What are your hours?

We're open Monday through Sunday, 10AM to 7PM. Saturday is our day of rest. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

Is parking available?

Yes! Free street parking is available near our spa. We're also accessible via the M/R subway lines for your convenience.

How do I book?

You can book online through our website, call us at (718) 841-9919, or use the contact form above. We'll confirm within 24 hours.

Payment methods?

We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards. Payment is due at the time of service. Gift cards are also available.

Cancellation policy?

We kindly request 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer the time to other clients.

Judgment-free space?

Absolutely! We welcome everyone regardless of age, size, gender, race, or background. Our spa is a safe, inclusive space.
