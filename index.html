Meet Laura

Laura's Beauty Touch was born from a simple belief: that beauty treatments should be as natural as they are luxurious. Founded by Laura, a passionate esthetician with over 15 years of experience, our spa has become a beloved sanctuary in Rego Park, Queens.

What began as a dream to create a space where clients could experience personalized care has blossomed into a thriving community. We've built our reputation on combining traditional techniques with modern innovation, always prioritizing your skin's health and your overall well-being.

Every treatment at Laura's Beauty Touch is thoughtfully curated, using organic products and professional expertise to deliver results that speak for themselves.
