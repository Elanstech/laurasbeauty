Meet Laura

Founder & Master Esthetician

Welcome to Laura's Beauty Touch, where natural beauty and expert care come together in perfect harmony. For over two decades, I've dedicated my life to helping people feel confident, radiant, and truly cared for.

As a New York State licensed skin care professional, I believe in the power of organic, pure ingredients and personalized attention. Every treatment is tailored to your unique needs, using only the finest natural products that honor both your skin and the environment.

My passion extends beyond skincare—from signature facials and aromatherapy to eyebrow microblading and eyelash extensions. Each service is a reflection of my commitment to making you look and feel your absolute best.

Our Philosophy

At Laura's Beauty Touch, we believe that true beauty comes from within and is nurtured through natural, holistic care. Our approach combines time-honored techniques with modern expertise, creating a personalized wellness experience that respects your body, mind, and spirit.

We're committed to sustainability, using organic ingredients and eco-friendly practices. Every product, every treatment, every moment is designed to bring you closer to your most radiant, confident self—naturally.
