Meet Laura

Founder & Master Esthetician

Welcome to Laura's Beauty Touch, where natural beauty and expert care come together in perfect harmony. For over two decades, I've dedicated my life to helping people feel confident, radiant, and truly cared for.

As a New York State licensed skin care professional, I believe in the power of organic, pure ingredients and personalized attention. Every treatment is customized to your unique needs, ensuring you receive exactly what your skin and body require.

What makes Laura's Beauty Touch special isn't just the treatments—it's the warm, inclusive environment we've created. This is a judgment-free zone where everyone is welcome, and every body is beautiful. My goal is simple: to make you feel good, inside and out.

Licensed Since 2001 New York State Board Certified
Continuing Education Advanced Training in Organic Skincare

Our Philosophy

At Laura's Beauty Touch, we believe beauty is more than skin deep. Our holistic approach combines professional expertise with natural, organic products to deliver treatments that nourish your body, calm your mind, and lift your spirit. We're not just about looking good—we're about feeling amazing from the inside out.

A Space For Everyone

We celebrate diversity and welcome all people, regardless of age, size, gender, race, or background. This is a safe, judgment-free zone where everyone deserves to feel beautiful and cared for.
